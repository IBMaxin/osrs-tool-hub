From Old School RuneScape Wiki

For melee equipment progression, see Guide:Melee Gear Progression. For magic equipment progression, see Guide:Magic Gear Progression.

This guide is a reference for ranged gear progression, both in effectiveness at different encounters and price. Upfront equipment cost as well as estimated ammo costs are compared, with a generic ranged strength bonus comparison below.
